About Us

The Cyber Alliance is a student-run club at RRU. The clubâ€™s main purpose is to prepare students for careers in private and public industries. To support students in their academic and career goals, the club organizes bi-weekly meetings where students are given technical and non-technical workshops/webinars to build their professional profiles and opportunities to get involved in teams to support operations within the Club. Moreover, the club will organize various CTF competitions for the students so that they can show-off their skills. Also, for the students who are new to the Cyber Security field, the club will organize CTF sessions, where students can learn and play CTFs along with the experienced CTF players. In addition, the Club will partner with the local/international companies and organizations to bring real-world experiences into the hands of students through trainings, community engagement, and job placement.

Mission Statement for The Cyber Alliance Club

Our mission is to promote awareness, education and advocacy in the field of cybersecurity and to foster a community of like-minded individuals who are committed to protecting themselves and others against cyber threats.

  • 01 Education

    We strive to provide resources and training to individuals and organizations on best practices for protecting their digital assets and information.

  • 02 Advocacy

    We advocate for policies that promote better cybersecurity practices and protections for individuals, businesses, and governments.

  • 03 Collaboration

    We encourage collaboration among our members and other cybersecurity professionals to share knowledge, ideas and strategies for combating cyber threats.

  • 03 Outreach

    We engage with the wider community to raise awareness about the importance of cybersecurity and to promote safe and responsible online behavior
